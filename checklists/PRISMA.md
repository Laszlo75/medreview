# PRISMA 2020 — systematic reviews & meta-analyses

Source: Page et al., *BMJ* 2021;372:n71. 27 items. Supersedes PRISMA 2009.
Reviewer: mark met / partial / unmet with location.

## Title
- **1** — Identified as a systematic review.

## Abstract
- **2** — Structured abstract (see PRISMA-for-Abstracts: objectives, eligibility,
  sources, methods, results, limitations, conclusions, registration).

## Introduction
- **3** — Rationale, in the context of existing knowledge.
- **4** — Explicit objectives / questions (e.g. PICO).

## Methods
- **5** — Eligibility criteria and how studies were grouped for synthesis.
- **6** — Information sources (databases, registers, dates last searched).
- **7** — Full search strategy for at least one database, reproducible.
- **8** — Selection process; how many reviewers, independence, any automation.
- **9** — Data-collection process; reviewers, independence, automation.
- **10a** — Outcomes sought; which results were sought from each study.
- **10b** — All other variables sought; assumptions about missing/unclear info.
- **11** — Risk-of-bias assessment: tool, number of assessors, independence.
- **12** — Effect measures used (e.g. RR, MD) for each outcome.
- **13a** — Processes to decide which studies were eligible for each synthesis.
- **13b** — Data preparation/handling before synthesis.
- **13c** — Methods to tabulate/display results.
- **13d** — Synthesis methods; if meta-analysis, model, heterogeneity
  (I²/τ²), software.
- **13e** — Methods to explore heterogeneity (subgroup, meta-regression).
- **13f** — Sensitivity analyses.
- **14** — Methods to assess **reporting bias / publication bias** across
  studies. *(High yield — often absent.)*
- **15** — Methods to assess **certainty of evidence** (e.g. GRADE).

## Results
- **16a** — Study selection numbers with a **flow diagram** (records →
  screened → excluded → included).
- **16b** — Studies excluded at full-text with reasons.
- **17** — Characteristics of each included study, with citation.
- **18** — Risk of bias per included study.
- **19** — Results of individual studies (effect estimate + precision).
- **20a** — For each synthesis, studies and their characteristics.
- **20b** — Synthesis results; if meta-analysis, summary estimate, precision,
  heterogeneity.
- **20c** — Investigations of possible causes of heterogeneity.
- **20d** — Sensitivity-analysis results.
- **21** — Reporting-bias assessments per synthesis.
- **22** — Certainty (GRADE) per outcome.

## Discussion
- **23a** — Interpretation in the context of other evidence.
- **23b** — Limitations of the evidence included.
- **23c** — Limitations of the review process.
- **23d** — Implications for practice, policy, and future research.

## Other
- **24a** — Registration name and number (e.g. PROSPERO), or that it wasn't
  registered.
- **24b** — Where the protocol can be accessed, or that none was prepared.
- **24c** — Amendments to registration/protocol.
- **25** — Support/funding and role of funders.
- **26** — Competing interests.
- **27** — Availability of data, code, and other materials
  (extracted data, analysis code).
